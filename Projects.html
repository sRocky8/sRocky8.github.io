Train of Bot

Train of Bot is a project I made for my class Advanced Game Workshop.

This project was made by five people. I was the programmer.

In this project I made an inventory system,
made NPCs speak different dialogue
based on what has happened in game, and more!

In this game you solve puzzles to stop the train
because Beep missed his stop.
